New York Fashion Week Highlights

Day 1: The Return of Bold Colors

The first day of NYFW didn't disappoint with designers embracing neon greens, electric blues, and fiery reds. Here are my top picks from the collections:

  • Marc Jacobs' oversized neon coats
  • Proenza Schouler's color-blocked dresses
  • Coach's playful pastel leather bags

Day 2: Sustainable Fashion Takes Center Stage

More designers than ever are committing to sustainable practices. Standout moments included:
